What is Ten Day Traveler?

We are not quitting our jobs to travel (as fun as that may sound), nor are we making a living off of our adventures. We work full time jobs and live busy lives. Even with this shortened time frame, traveling is a huge part of our lifestyle. We have 10 days to explore a new part of the world. That may not sound like a lot of time, but it can be enough to have an amazing adventure. We are here to share the tips and tricks of how to make the most of your numbered vacation days. Who are we?

We are sisters and travel soulmates.

Meet Sarah:

Sarah is the planner, organizer, and the brains behind our iteneraries. She loves research and finding unique places to visit (and great flight deals!). With a Master's in business from Northwestern, Sarah has had the opportunity to study in countries all over the world including Isreal, Vietnam, Sri Lanka, and Spain. She even worked for the US embassy in Argentia! When she's not traveling she enjoys marathon training and reading.

Meet Emily:

Emily is the resident photographer behind our journeys. She brings a carefree spirit to our trips, being the perfect counterpart to Sarah's planning and research. She loves travel because it allows her to meet new people and see new places. A former professional photographer, Emily now works as a Software Engineer.
